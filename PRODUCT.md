# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Primary users are computer-vision researchers, lab students, and practitioners using the tool for dataset annotation and model debugging. Secondary users include open-source contributors who run the app locally to prepare datasets.

## Product Purpose

InoLabel is a desktop annotation application for images and video that supports tracking, standard detection, oriented bounding boxes (OBB), and classification. Its purpose is to provide a fast, reliable, and user-friendly environment for creating and exporting labeled datasets for computer vision workflows.

Success looks like stable local runs on common platforms (Windows/Linux), clear and discoverable annotation workflows, reliable export to YOLO/COCO formats, and low friction for students and researchers to produce high-quality labeled data.

## Brand Personality

friendly, practical, research-focused

## Anti-references

- Not a marketing-first landing site; avoid large hero marketing patterns and generic SaaS cosmetics.
- Avoid the "," all-caps eyebrow-heavy landing page grammar; prefer clear, tool-focused UI.

## Design Principles

- Make the core workflow obvious: reduce clicks from data→annotate→export.
- Prioritize clarity and legibility: readable typography, strong contrast, and consistent visual hierarchy.
- Minimize accidental data loss: visible autosave, easy undo/redo, clear export confirmations.
- Optimize for responsiveness and keyboard-driven workflows used by power users and students.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target WCAG AA for core UI screens (contrast, keyboard navigation, focus states).
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for animations; provide reduced-motion fallbacks.
- Ensure color choices consider common color-blindness modes and do not rely on color alone to convey status.
